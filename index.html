Minecraft

Players explore a procedurally generated, three-dimensional world with virtually infinite terrain made up of voxels. Players can discover and extract raw materials, craft tools and items, and build structures, earthworks, and machines.

  • I can also add my own codes to the game to modify it.
  • Playing with friends is also an option for this game.
  • Minecraft can also be played offline.
  • There are also various gamemodes that you can play alone or with friends.
